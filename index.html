About

We know all work and no play is bad, but there is no reason to not try merging the two. Who's clues is a playable encyclopedia that allows users to play figures from history while learning about simultaneously learning about their contributions to the world as we know it.
In this day and age when online learning has had to quickly become the norm, students have been fraught with issues regarding motivation and concentration. Who's clues combines education with gaming, a blend the creators personally think is very important right now to help with focus while also easing the burden of rote learning.
In the future, we hope to expand to add a large number of personalities into our database so it can also aid as a quick lookup.
